My Story

As the co-founder of Divine Species, alongside Steven Toth and Gib Jenkins , Martin Leyhe had a vision for bringing two of his greatest passions, art and music, together. Born in Oakland, CA and raised in Wisconsin, The young artist drew inspiration from the nature that surrounded him, as well as, big city landscapes, graffiti and street artists he came across in the bay. Creating a visual aesthetic that is lively, playful, vibrant, and true to his roots, Martin uses a combination of various mediums to create different effects on his audience, some pieces loud and in your face while others are calm and collected. Gaining inspiration from the color and movement found in nature's organic objects, Leyhe has been busy creating graffiti art and cunning visuals, always finding a unique way to please the eyes and senses.
